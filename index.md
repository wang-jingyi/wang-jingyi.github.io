#### Research Interest

In general, I am interested in *developing principled methodologies towards formal trust assurance of complex intelligent systems integrating software, AI and control*, covering the following research topics:
- Formal reasoning of safety and security (see [TSE 2021](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/8576657), [CCS 2025 a](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3719027.3765138), [CCS 2025 b](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3719027.3765057), [ICSE 2025](https://www.computer.org/csdl/proceedings-article/icse/2025/056900a734/251mHfUjxMk), [WWW 2025](https://dl.acm.org/doi/abs/10.1145/3696410.3714682), etc);
- Testing, verification and repair of AI models/applications/systems, e.g., deep neural networks (DNNs, including large language models) (see [ICSE 2019](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/8812047), [ICSE 2020](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3377811.3380331), [ICSE 2021](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1109/ICSE43902.2021.00038), [TACAS 2021](https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-3-030-72016-2_21), [ICSE 2022](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3510003.3510123), [ICSE 2024](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3597503.3623332), [ISSTA 2024](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3650212.3652132), and [ASE 2025](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/11334285), etc);
- AI safety, security and ethics, e.g., for DNNs or DNN-based systems/applications (see [S&P 2022](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/9833747), [TDSC 2024](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/10480645), [NDSS 2026](), [WWW 2026](), etc);


Collaborations are warmly welcome from both academia or industry!
